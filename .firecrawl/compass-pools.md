### Acrylic or Disappearing Edges

Disappearing edges (also known as vanishing edges or [infinity pools](https://www.compasspools.com.au/news/build-a-beautiful-infinity-pool/)) are one of the most asked-about pool customisations. If you plan to have an [above-ground pool](https://www.compasspools.com.au/above-ground-pools/) then this could certainly be an option for you. If you are considering a disappearing edge on your pool then it pays to do your research.

Infinity edges require a catchment tank as well as extra pumps and they can be noisy. In recent years we have developed acrylic edge options. Acrylic edges allow you to create the same effect as a vanishing edge, but with fewer downsides; no water overflowing from the pool edge means no need for additional tanks or extra pumps.
